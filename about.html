About Angel Food And Spices

Angel Food and Spices is your one-stop shop for premium quality Nigerian food ingredients and party souvenirs. We specialize in offering a wide range of dried local delicacies, including dried ewedu, dried ponmo ijebu, dried goat meat, dried cow meat, snail (igbin), dried iru, and dried ugwu. Our expertly crafted pepper mixes, such as ofada pepper mix and agonyi pepper mix, bring authentic Nigerian flavors to your kitchen.

We also provide essential party souvenirs like rice and garri, perfect for weddings, celebrations, and special events. At Angel Food and Spices, we pride ourselves on providing high-quality ofada rice, palm oil, oil, iru spices, and pepper soup spices, ensuring every meal is bursting with flavor.
